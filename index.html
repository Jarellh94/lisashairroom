Services

Natural Hair

Your natural hair can be the most: Beautiful, Luxurious, Healthy, Frustrating, Painful, Occasionally time consuming yet rewarding experience. And my goal is to make it the best experience possible. ​

Short Styles

Short styles are great for those whom want to make a dramatic statement. Short styles do require frequent touch ups on the cut to keep style looking its best.​

Press-N-Curl

You have to try it to believe it! Get the look of a relaxer without the chemical damage and unbelievable body. ​Not your mom's Press-N-Curl.​

Up Do's

Up Do's are perfect for warm weather, low maintenance, formal events and in between stages of hair growth.​

Locs

Locs are very versatile especially the longer they become. They can hold their own against most hair cuts and non Loc styles. Getting through the starter Loc stage is tough but it's my job to make it as painless as possible.

Weaves

Weaves are great for protecting your own hair when going natural, giving your hair a styling break, trying a new color or style, adding length, or trouble with alopecia.​

Children

I love getting such a fresh start with a young girl's hair journey. I accept girls age 6 and up. I have a light touch and believe in the comfort and health of a person's hair and scalp.

Relaxed Hair

Relaxers have gotten a lot of bad press lately but my clients that are relaxed for the reason of texturing or tender scalp have had success with growth and hair health as long as they stick to the regiment I have put in place specifically for them. Most importantly, relaxers are NOT for everyone and should always be done by a professional. ​

Braids

Braids can be a benefit if done correctly. Braid styles are good for vacation, protective styles, and in between stages of length. Yet if not maintained properly, or left in too long, can cause major shedding, breakage, and sometimes hair loss.
